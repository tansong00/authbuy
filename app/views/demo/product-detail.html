Jani Kaskinen, Chief Executive Officer of Hotelzon International

VANs were chosen because they offers greater control and security when making payments to hotel suppliers. The platform enables our customers to set the date and amount of the virtual card transaction and also allows multiple authorisation messages, which is a unique feature of the solution.

Adopting VANs is helping us to streamline and secure the way we handle pre-paid bookings made by customers at our partner hotels.
